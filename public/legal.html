Terms of Service

Welcome to SOVEREIGN. By registering an account and playing the game, you agree to abide by the following terms.

  • Account Ownership: You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. You may only operate one (1) nation per person.
  • No Botting or Automation: The use of macros, scripts, bots, or third-party software to automate gameplay is strictly prohibited.
  • Fair Play: Exploiting bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics for an advantage is forbidden. If you discover an exploit, report it immediately.
  • Account Termination: The administrators of SOVEREIGN reserve the right to ban, suspend, or delete any account at any time for violation of these rules, without prior warning.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This section outlines how we handle your data.

  • Information Collection: We collect only the information provided during registration (username, email address) and gameplay data generated during your session.
  • Data Usage: Your email address is used solely for account recovery and critical system broadcasts. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use browser local storage (specifically JSON Web Tokens) to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
  • Security: All passwords are cryptographically hashed using bcrypt. We cannot view your plaintext password.

User Conduct

SOVEREIGN is a competitive geopolitical game, but we expect players to maintain basic decency.

  • Harassment, real-world threats, doxxing, or severe hate speech in in-game messages or alliance chat will result in immediate bans.
  • Nation names, city names, and leader names must not contain highly offensive or illegal content.
  • In-game espionage, warfare, and political backstabbing are expected mechanics and do not constitute harassment unless they cross into real-world abuse.
